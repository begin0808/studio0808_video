SRT Subtitle Editor Guide

01. Purpose

The SRT Subtitle Editor is a lightweight, efficient proofreading tool. It is specifically designed to handle AI-generated subtitles, allowing you to quickly correct typos and adjust timecodes without opening heavy video editing software.

02. Key Features

  • Pagination System: To prevent lag when handling long videos, this tool uses "pagination" technology. It displays 50 entries per page, ensuring a fast and smooth editing experience.
  • Dual-Column Editing: Timecodes (start and end) are on the left, and text content is on the right. You can edit entries directly in the grid.
  • Instant Save: Click the "Save" button at the top to merge all changes across all pages and write them back to the original .srt file.

03. Pro Tips

💡 How to switch pages?

Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons at the top or bottom of the list. Don't worry, the program automatically caches your current changes when you switch pages until you hit the final "Save" button.

💡 Timecode Format

Standard time formats must follow 00:00:00,000 (HH:MM:SS,ms). If the format is invalid, players might not be able to read that specific subtitle segment correctly.
